Dirt Pudding

Description

Dirt pudding is a fun and delicious no-bake dessert that looks like real dirt but tastes like chocolate heaven! Made with layers of creamy chocolate pudding, crushed Oreo 'dirt,' and topped with gummy worms, it’s a playful treat loved by kids and adults alike

Ingredients(per Serving)

  • 3 cups milk
  • 2 (3.5 ounce) packages instant French vanilla pudding mix
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • ¼ cup butter, softened
  • 1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 1 (14 ounce) package chocolate cream-filled sandwich cookies, crushed (divided)

Steps

  1. Get all ingredients together
  2. Whisk milk and pudding mix in a large bowl until softly set, about 2 minutes. The pudding will thicken as it stands
  3. Beat confectioners' sugar, cream cheese, and butter in a separate bowl until smooth and creamy.
  4. Stir cream cheese mixture into pudding until thoroughly combined; gently fold in frozen whipped topping
  5. Place 1/2 of the cookie crumbs into the bottom of a 9x12-inch dish; spread evenly
  6. Cover cookie crumbs with pudding mixture and top with remaining crumbs, covering completely.
  7. Chill for at least 30 minutes before serving.
