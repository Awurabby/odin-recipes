Banana Pancake

Description

A pancake is a soft, round, and delicious flat cake made from a smooth batter of flour, eggs, milk, and butter.

Ingredients(per Serving)

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Â¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed

Steps

  1. Get all ingredients together
  2. In one bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. In a separate bowl, blend the egg, milk, vegetable oil, and mashed bananas. Gradually combine the dry ingredients with the banana mixture, stirring until the batter is slightly lumpy.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Cook until pancakes are golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes per side. Serve hot
  6. Serve hot and enjoy
